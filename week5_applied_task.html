This Week's Task
Before your next visit — write it down. After — check it.

Pick one location you're visiting this week. Before you go, write down every person you remember from the last visit — their name or dog's name, what you talked about, and where you left off. Then walk in and use at least one of those threads. After the visit, come back here and reflect on what changed.
