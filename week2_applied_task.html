The Mission

Plan one full recruiting day the evening before. Then compare.

  1. Pick a recruiting day this week — ideally one of your heavier days.
  2. The evening before: call or check in on 3–5 locations. Find out what's actually there. Build your route around the intel, not a habit.
  3. Go out the next day with a specific first stop, a reason for each location, and at least one name to ask for at your best prospects.
  4. At the end of that day, fill out the report below. Bring it to Monday's meeting.
Guardrails
  • Keep the pre-planning to 20 minutes or less — this isn't about adding work, it's about spending the same time more intentionally.
  • If you can't call ahead, use whatever intel you already have. Known patterns count.
  • If the planned day falls apart (it will sometimes), note what adapted well and what you'd do differently.
